# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting
Report concerns through the maintainers listed in `SECURITY.md` until a dedicated conduct contact is established.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or reject content and participation that violates this code of conduct.
